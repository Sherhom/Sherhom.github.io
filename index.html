Project

Research on Tamper-Proof Cloud Forensic Framework (Leader)

Cloud forensics has become more and more critical in the field of cloud computing security in recent years. A fundamental problem in cloud forensics lies in how to obtain, preserve, and analyze evidence safely and effectively. We developed a new Cloud Forensic Tamper-Proof Framework (TamForen) of cloud forensic, which is used in an untrusted and multi-tenancy cloud environment. TamForen will take into account the untrustworthiness of participants in the forensics process and conduct tamper-proof protection of data in a decentralized way without violating users' privacy.

Cloud Security    Cloud Forensics    Provenance Data    Distribution System

Research on Nanjing University Public Opinion Monitoring (Leader)

Public opinion monitoring is a hot topic at present, through the automatic acquisition of massive information on the Internet, automatic classification and clustering, and topic detection to achieve the user's network public opinion monitoring and news topic tracking and other information needs. We conduct public opinion monitoring on the relevant topics of universities on ZhiHu, collect relevant information in time, analyze the trend of public opinion, and monitor the public opinion of Nanjing University in real time based on Spark.

Design and Implementation of DL-based Abnormal Behavior Detection System (Leader)

With the development of modern society, the classroom behavior of students has become an important factor restricting the effective development of higher education. “Abnormal behaviors”are behaviors that breach the norms of classroom. We implemented a classroom abnormal behavior detection system. The system mainly includes the following four parts: video stream processing, student human target detection, abnormal behavior recognition, and visualization of recognition result data.
